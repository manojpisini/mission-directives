# Rollback and Recovery Plan Template

> **Purpose:** Define preconditions, backups, rollback steps, verification, residuals, and escalation.

## Template operating rules

1. Replace every bracketed field with verified content or an explicit `Not Applicable` / `Unknown` record.
2. Preserve source lineage for material claims, commands, numbers, defaults, and compatibility statements.
3. Do not delete required sections; mark them not applicable with a reason when they genuinely do not apply.
4. Keep one canonical source for repeated facts and link to it rather than copying divergent text.
5. Add backlinks to the parent hub and forward links to prerequisites, next actions, references, and troubleshooting.
6. Verify links, anchors, commands, examples, schemas, exports, and accessibility before completion.
